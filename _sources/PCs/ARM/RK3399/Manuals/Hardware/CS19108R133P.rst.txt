Mounting Procedure
==================

|Product| can be mounted with 4 x M4 screws using the VESA (100x100cm or 75x75cm) and Panel mounting methods, enabling simplified installation onto any standard mounting fixture.

.. attention::

    Please make sure the display is not exposed to high pressure when mounting into an enclosure.

You can find detailed information about mounting in the |mguide|.
