HDMI Input¶

The product has an HDMI input port, you can play video in another device and let this product to display it on the large screen.

Charging¶

The product can be charged through a 12V DC charger, please use the charger shipped with the product to charge this product.

Buttons¶

The buttons on the product are: power on/off, volume, volume-.

Dimension¶

The dimension of the screen is 525.9 * 293.8 * 15 mm, with the base stand, the height is horizontal: 1140.5 mm, vertical: 1252.5 mm.
