    Power InputÂ¶

    The AIO-PX30-101 Industrial PC can be powered by a wide range of input voltages: From 9V to 36V DC. The total power consumption is typically about 6W. The power input connector is a 3.4/1.7mm DC connector. For a proper DC power adapter, refer to the figure below. We also provide a customzied power cable of 3.4/1.7mm to 5.5/2.1mm for customer.

    Buttons and Status LEDÂ¶

    Status LEDÂ¶

    This product has an LED status indicator on the backside, as the figure below shows. The LED turns GREEN when the device is turned on and flashes YELLOW when the CPU is working.

    ButtonsÂ¶

    There are three (3) buttons on the backside of the case: Volume +, Volume -, and Power, as the figure below shows.

    The AIO-PX30-101 Industrial PC will boot automaticlly after power on, donât need to press Power button. After system booted, we can use Power button to sleep and awake system.

    ConnectivityÂ¶

    There are many connectivity options available on the AIO-PX30-101 industrial PC. It has 2 x USB 2.0 Host, 1 x mini USB OTG, 1 x Channel 100Mbps LAN (RJ45) Ethernet connector supporting up to 100Mbps, and RS232+RS485+CAN+Relay connector.

    USB HOST and USB OTGÂ¶

    There are 2 x Type A USB HOST connectors and 1 x USB-OTG Connector onboard, as shown on the figures below.

    Note

    • External USB peripherals like a USB disk or USB mouse or keyboard can connect to the USB HOST.

    • You can download software to the eMMC using the USB OTG.

    LAN ConnectorsÂ¶

    LAN (RJ45) connector provides Ethernet connectivity over standardized Ethernet cables as shown the figure below. The integrated Ethernet interface supports up to 100Mbps data throughput.

    Note

    Use CAT5 or better cables to achieve full data throughput over maximum distance defined by the 1000BASE-T standard (100m).

    TF Card and SIM Card SlotsÂ¶

    The AIO-PX30-101 Industrial PC features 1 x TF Card (micro SD) slot and 1 x SIM Card slot. You can use the SIM card slot when you mount the 4G/LTE module.

    Note

    1. The product does not come shipped with the TF Card and the SIM Card by default.

    ZIGBEE ModuleÂ¶

    The AIO-PX30-101 Industrial PC supports an onboard Zigbee module. The Zigbee controller is TI CC2531, and the Raspberry Pi forum supports it.

    Attention
